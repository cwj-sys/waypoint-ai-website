About Waypoint AI

We exist to close the gap between small businesses and AI that actually works.

Waypoint AI is a Guelph-based consulting firm that helps small and medium businesses across Southwest Ontario implement practical AI — at a price that makes sense, in plain language, with results you can see in weeks.

Our Mission

AI should work for every business, not just the ones with IT departments.

The businesses that make your community run — the trades, the professional services firms, the retailers, the service providers — are falling behind on AI, not because they don't care, but because the tools are confusing and the advice available to them is built for enterprises with six-figure technology budgets.

Waypoint AI exists to change that. We take the same AI technology that large corporations are deploying at scale and make it practical, affordable, and accessible for businesses with 5 to 100 employees.

We don't sell platforms, subscriptions, or vague strategy documents. We build things that work, explain how they work in plain language, and hand them over so your team can use them without thinking about AI at all.

Our Technology Choice

We build exclusively on Claude.

Most AI consultancies try to be platform-agnostic — a little ChatGPT here, a little Google Gemini there, whatever the client asks for. We've made a different choice: we build exclusively on Claude, Anthropic's AI platform.

That's not because other platforms aren't capable. It's because deep expertise in one best-in-class tool produces better results than shallow familiarity with a dozen. Our clients benefit from that depth every time.

Claude is consistently ranked among the leading AI models for the type of work that matters most to businesses: writing, reasoning, following complex instructions, and handling long documents reliably. Anthropic, the company behind Claude, was founded with AI safety as a core mission — which has practical implications for business use.

Claude AI

The core language model powering all our automations — for writing, analysis, document processing, and reasoning tasks.

Claude Cowork

Anthropic's desktop automation platform — used for building workflows that work alongside your team's existing tools and processes.

Claude Code

Used for building custom integrations that connect AI to your existing software — CRM, accounting tools, email, and more.

Enterprise-grade API access with no training use of your data — the foundation for all client automations we build.
