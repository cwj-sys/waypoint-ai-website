Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Still on the fence? These are the questions we hear most often.

Do I need any technical knowledge to work with you?
Not at all. We handle all the technical work. You just need to tell us where your business is losing time or money — we'll figure out the rest and explain everything in plain language as we go.
What kinds of businesses do you work with?
Any small or medium business with 2–100 employees. We've worked with tradespeople, professional services firms, retailers, and healthcare admin teams. If you have repetitive tasks, paperwork, or customer communication — there's almost certainly something we can automate.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a general tool you have to figure out yourself. We build purpose-built solutions that are wired into your specific workflows, trained on your business context, and handed off so your team can use them without thinking about the AI at all. It's the difference between a hammer and a custom-built workbench.
What if I don't know what I want automated?
That's exactly what the AI Readiness Audit is for. We spend time with you to surface the opportunities you haven't spotted yet — most business owners are surprised by what's possible. Start there, no commitment required.
Do I own what you build?
Yes, 100%. Everything we build belongs to you. There's no licensing, no lock-in, and no ongoing fees unless you choose to continue with a retainer.
Is my business data safe?
Yes. We use Anthropic's Claude platform, which has enterprise-grade privacy and security practices. We'll walk you through exactly how your data is handled before any project starts, and we can sign an NDA if needed.
